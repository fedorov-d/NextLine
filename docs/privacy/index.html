NextLine Transit Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

NextLine Transit helps users browse transit stops, routes, and live arrival information.

To provide those features, the app connects to third-party transit and infrastructure services.

Information We Access

NextLine Transit may access your device location while you use the app in order to show nearby transit stops and improve map-based browsing. Location access is optional and can be changed at any time in your device settings.

Information We Store

The app may store preferences such as favorite stops on your device so features like favorites, widgets, and Apple Watch support can work properly.

Third-party services used by the app may also store service data needed to respond to requests, such as transit request records, cached responses, and system logs, subject to their own privacy practices.

Information We Collect

We do not sell your personal information.

When you use the app, third-party services the app connects to may receive the information needed to respond to app requests and provide their services.

If you contact support directly, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message in order to respond and troubleshoot your issue.

We use collected operational data only for service delivery, maintenance, security, reliability, abuse prevention, and support.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on third-party transit and infrastructure services to deliver network-based features. Those services may process app request data under their own terms and privacy policies.

The app also relies on Apple platform services such as widgets, Apple Watch connectivity, and system location services. Those services may process data as required to provide their functionality.

Data Retention

Data stored locally in the app, such as favorites, remains on your device unless you remove it or delete the app.

Third-party services the app uses may retain service data according to their own retention practices. Support communications you send directly to us may be retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond, troubleshoot, and meet legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can remove stored favorites from within the app.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of support communications you sent directly to us, subject to legal or operational retention requirements.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and uninstall it from your device.

Children's Privacy

NextLine Transit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

Contact
